How it works
From syllabus to study plan
in under 60 seconds.

No more hours of planning. ProNotes reads your syllabus and does the thinking so you can focus on the actual studying.

STEP 01 📤

Upload your syllabus

Drop in your PDF, Word doc, or paste your course outline. ProNotes reads every topic, deadline, and exam date automatically.

STEP 02 🤖

AI builds your plan

Our AI maps out a day-by-day study schedule tailored to your exam dates, workload, and available hours. It even adapts if you fall behind.

STEP 03 🚀

Study, track, ace it

Follow your plan, flip through AI flashcards, and watch your progress climb. Show up to every exam knowing you are ready.
